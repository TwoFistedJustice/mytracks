outside [Rogers
Camp](http://www.riderplanet-usa.com/atv/trails/info/oregon_01711/ride_c781.htm)
in the Tillamook Forest. I started and ended the ride at [See See Motor Coffee
Co](http://www.seeseemotorcycles.com/).
